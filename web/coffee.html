The Bench

Step behind the bar — your cup, your pace.

Each step hands you a tool — the glowing one. Grab it, take your time, redo anytime.

Pick a bean, then crank to grind.

Watch the beans tip into the hopper, then turn the crank until the basket fills to the band — sparse pulls weak, packed too tight turns bitter.
Bean
An empty basket, ready to fill.

Hold the lever on the machine to pull.

Short of the sweet spot? Hold again — the cup tops up where it left off, so you can creep right into the pocket.
An empty cup, ready for the shot.

Pick a milk, then hold the steam wand.

Hold again to keep texturing. Stop while it's glossy and silky — pushed too hot, the milk scalds and the foam stiffens.
Milk
Cold milk, ready to steam.

Hold on the cup, wiggle to draw the leaf.

Pour again to top up where you left off. When it's near the rim, pick a finish and stir it through to serve.
The shot's in — milk next.

Stir it through to serve.
